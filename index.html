Crystal Collectors

Instructions


You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score. Win the game by matching your total score with the randomly generated number!

But be careful! Each crystal is worth a different amount of points as you click on them. And you won't be able to see their values until you click each one! And all of these values change each time the game restarts.
